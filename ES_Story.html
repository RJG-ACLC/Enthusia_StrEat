Product Philosophy

Enthusia StrEat is a small food stall that sell fruitshakes, burgers and lots of common meriendas that is popular amongst Filipino people. With their delectable fruitshake, made with fresh ingredients that they prepare right in front of you. Alongside, a fresh beef patty on the Hamburger, filled with an appetizing secret sauce. Enthusia StrEat also offers different Filipino meriendas, that they prepare the same day as they sell it. Enthusia StrEat is known for their fresh ingredients, with their self-formulated and handmade beef patties, right here in Barangay Tatalon. And they proudly say that, all of their products, are made with love and purity.
